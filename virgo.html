Virgo

August 23rd - September 22nd








One of the 88 constellations recognized by the International Astronomical Union is the Constellation of Virgo. Despite being a distinct sign from the constellation of Virgo, it contains a few stars that are still in the sign of Libra. The sixth sign occupies 30 degrees of the zodiac's circular route, lying between Leo and Libra. This fluctuating indicator heralds the coming of autumn. It's extremely depressing because of how constantly it changes. It represents the transition from the lovely summer to the allegedly gloomy fall when everything disappears.


To make matters worse, the Sun sets in the sign of Libra, which is Virgo's predecessor. Spica, whose name translates to "ear of grain," originally appears in Babylonian astronomy between 1000 and 700 BC. The constellation was once known as "The Furrow," and its name, Spica, comes from the Latin for "ear of grain." It symbolizes the goddess Shala's ear of grain. This represents an outstanding effort that produces positive results. Virgo is frequently seen with a stalk of wheat. This sign is focused on thinking and engaging with inner awareness because a Virgo woman or man's solar return created it.


Virgin represents the readiness and utility of late summer/early fall activities, and those with Virgin as their rising, sun, or moon sign possess these qualities in the heart of their characters. Virgo is a changeable sign and an alchemical "synthesizer," able to integrate disparate, unrelated elements to create a meaningful, practical harmony. People with the Virgin sign in their natal charts excel in dedicating themselves to projects and connections that require care, healing, and upkeep. The main characteristics of Virgo are mental flexibility, adaptability, and meticulousness. These people are conscientious and watchful, have sharp minds, pick up latest information quickly, and feel intense satisfaction from administering medicine and managing their surroundings. They are normally calm and considerate and are adept at saying what is useful and what is not.


They are frequently humble and modest because they are too focused on improving themselves and the world around them to become pretentious or egotistical. Virgos have excellent communication skills, and they value developing a useful emotional vocabulary to help them and others understand one another. They take pleasure in helping their friends and family, not in a self-centered way but in a naturally attentive and kind one. People with Virgin birth signs are talented in both the arts and sciences.


The best traits of Virgo could also be its worst traits. This remarkably effective mind can easily become hyperactive despite having a superb brain and mental agility, which can cause worry, anxiety, and insomnia. These indigenous must stay rooted in their bodies, their breath, and the present moment because whatever they conjure up can end up being more real than reality itself. A Virgin may develop an obsession with helping others if they identify with it too strongly. It's important to keep in mind that it's okay sometimes to "simply be," as well as to strike a balance between helping others and engaging in some healthy self-care and "healthy selfishness." They may be exerting themselves to escape uncomfortable feelings.
