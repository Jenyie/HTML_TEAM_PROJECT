Libra

September 23rd - October 22nd








The constellation does not correspond to the zodiacal sign of Libra. Between Virgo and Scorpio, this sign is the sixth in the zodiac. The beginning of autumn is signaled by the cardinal sign of Libra. It's not unexpected that this area is associated with death because it symbolizes the natural cycle during which everything dies down, goes into hibernation, and enables fall to arrive.


Since it is a portion of the sign of Scorpio and symbolizes the claws of the Scorpion, the constellation of Libra does not actually exist in the conventional sense. In Babylonian astrology, it was referred as as MUL Zibanu and represented both the balance scales and the claws of a scorpion. Ancient Greeks referred to it in the same way. In actuality, the 15th degree of the Libra sign symbolizes a complete state of equilibrium. The stars that make up the other points in this constellation really belong to another constellation. The dynamic, masculine, or yang characteristics that define Libra are what make this sign more likely to engage with the outside world.


Those with equanimous energy at their core are those born beneath the sun, rising moon, or moon. Because Libra is a cardinal air sign, it possesses friendliness and leadership skills. People with Libra have a natural capacity to organize their team, family, or neighborhood and to initiate social projects and gatherings. The planet of attraction and pleasure rules the Scales, which makes them excellent diplomats skilled in bringing about peace and mediating disputes. Because Libra is ruled by Venus, the planet of attraction and pleasure, it has a propensity to be forgiving and willing to make amends so that everyone might regain their appreciation for life's finer things.


The positive traits of "Venus" are closely related to the qualities of "Libra." These people will be able to put other people's comfort and well-being first for the good of all. They are good communicators who aim for fairness in all discussions, even when conflicting points of view are involved. The negative traits of "Venus" are closely related to the qualities of "Libra." All of Libra's positive traits have an equal number of drawbacks and potential blind spots. Contrary to their polar Aries colleagues, Librans have trouble relating to their fiercely autonomous natures in a relational sense. Given that the sun is in Libra, where it is in its "dive," there is a risk that the situation for these residents will deteriorate. For the sake of keeping the peace, they sometimes will not openly express their annoyance or anger, but if they do not take precautions, this could result in repressed feelings that could turn into resentment. As Libra's Venusian focus on the nice and pleasant will divert their attention away from their more unpleasant sentiments, this ill will may later manifest in peculiar ways.
