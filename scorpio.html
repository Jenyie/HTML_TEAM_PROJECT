Scorpio

October 23rd - November 21st








Scorpios are found in the southern hemisphere of the night sky between Libra and Sagittarius. They have a right elevation of 16 hours and 30 minutes and a declination of 30 degrees south. Both constellations in the skies, Scorpio and Orion, were made by Zeus. One of the more important constellations in ancient astrology is the Scorpion. The constellation of Scorpius contains a number of bright stars, including Antares, one of the brightest stars in the sky. It also houses the stinger of its namesake, the bright star Acrab, which denotes the Scorpion's head.


As a water sign, Scorpio is known for its representatives' empathy and outward displays of emotion. People with this solar sign's deep emotional identification with the planets and their depth of analytical understanding make them adept at reading between the lines. Until they find something they are passionate about and can devote all their time, energy, and emotion to, they consider themselves restless souls. Nothing can stop a Scorpio from achieving their goals once they are set. They are incredibly resourceful, and once they decide on something, they will do whatever it takes to make it happen. Their focus, commitment, and keen natures are the hallmarks of the Scorpio sign. They put in a lot of effort, are frequently inventive, and have a strong passion for learning and studying. The Scorpion is dependable in that they follow through on all its commitments.


The strongest qualities of the Scorpio archetype might occasionally be its weakest ones. Even if they are persistent and committed to their work, they tend to become so inflexible, fixed, and stubborn that it might be challenging for them to change with the times or drop an outdated idea. While Scorpios are sensitive and tolerant, they can also be wary and careful around other people. Others may be more honest about their issues, but Scorpions may interpret what others have said to them as emotional manipulation and control if they are worried. Those who have wronged the Scorpion may experience his or her wrath because they tend to take longer than the other fixed signs to forgive them.
